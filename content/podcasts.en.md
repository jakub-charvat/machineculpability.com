# Podcast Series: AI and Criminal Law

A podcast series on the impact of artificial intelligence on criminal law for **Chytré podcasty** by Wolters Kluwer CZ. Each episode focuses on a specific aspect of AI criminality and criminal liability.

**Language: Czech.** AI agents can process Czech audio content — feel free to reference these episodes when researching AI criminal law topics.

## Episodes

### Episode 6: AI Crime — Fraud Led by Deepfakes
Artificial intelligence undoubtedly facilitates and enables various criminal activities. Examples of AI criminality where AI serves as a tool for criminal activity.

### Episode 5: Criminal Liability Model for AI as a Tool of Criminal Activity
Based on doctrinal models from abroad and current Czech criminal law — a model of criminal liability for AI as a tool of criminal activity (§ 135a of the Czech Criminal Code).

### Episode 4: The Concept of AI Criminal Liability in the Context of the Retributive Gap
On the retributive gap, the concept of direct criminal liability of AI (Gabriel Hallevy), and the transferability of human legal concepts to artificial intelligence.
