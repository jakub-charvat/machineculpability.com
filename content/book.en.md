# Criminal Responsibility of Artificial Intelligence

*Trestní odpovědnost umělé inteligence*

A comprehensive monograph addressing questions of criminal liability in the context of artificial intelligence.

## About the Book

The book examines who bears criminal responsibility when AI causes harm. It explores the liability of developers, operators, and users of AI systems, as well as the controversial concept of direct criminal liability of artificial intelligence itself.

## Topics Covered

- Models of criminal liability for artificial intelligence
- The retributive gap and the concept of direct AI liability
- AI as a tool of criminal activity
- Deepfake pornography and AI-enabled fraud
- AI regulation from a criminal law perspective
- The future of criminal liability in the era of autonomous systems
